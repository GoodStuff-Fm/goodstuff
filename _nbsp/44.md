##
Episode № 44
Jeremy Keith


November 19th, 2013
&middot;
89
minutes


Our talk with Jeremy runs the gamut of the web - progressive enhancement, depending on a database, sirens, the death of web services, the telegraph, transcriptions, CERN and preparing for a great talk.


**Guest Starring:**
