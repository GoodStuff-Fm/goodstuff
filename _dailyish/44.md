##
Episode № 44
Surviving Survivor for 29 Seasons


September 26th, 2014
&middot;
16
minutes


Chris Enns is joined by Susan Enns to chat about the start of season 29 of Survivor. Why do they still watch it after all these years? What do they think about this season&rsquo;s guests, the show format and Jeff Probst&rsquo;s silly questions?


**Spoiler warning**: We will be talking about the results from Survivor so don&rsquo;t listen until after you&rsquo;ve watched if you&rsquo;re at all worried about that.
