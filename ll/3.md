##
Episode № 3
The Black Episode


October 7th, 2011
&middot;
30
minutes


In episode 3, we do a quick review of Wunderlisting and chatting about pushing the limits of what you think you can do. And then the colour black takes over. Black websites, black business cards and black turtlenecks.


We touch a bit on A/B testing your website.
