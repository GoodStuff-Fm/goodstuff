Episode № 16
What Happened to the the Days When You Got Naked in Person?


December 12th, 2013
&middot;
61
minutes


We talk about charging for consulting and using your expertise to help others while possibly making a buck. We also talk about Instagram and Twitter adding private photo messaging – and try and figure out Snapchat.
