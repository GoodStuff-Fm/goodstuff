Episode № 80
Caleb Wojcik - Doing It On Video


April 7th, 2015
&middot;
76
minutes


Caleb Wojcik runs DIY Video Guy - a audio podcast and video podcast show teaching  what he&rsquo;s learned about running a business involved in video production.  We talk about charging money for your work, focusing on clients vs marketing your podcast, and the value of dipping your toes into video podcasting if you&rsquo;ve only ever done audio.
