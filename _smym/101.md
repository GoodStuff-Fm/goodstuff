Episode № 101
Sean Kheraj - The Web is Shifting Ground Beneath Your Feet


September 15th, 2015
&middot;
56
minutes


Sean Kheraj hosts Nature&rsquo;s Past a podcast about environmental history research in Canada featuring lectures and interviews with authors and academics on the greatest country in the world - Canada, naturally. We talk about scheduling guests, recording groups discussions and using YouTube as a podcast platform.
