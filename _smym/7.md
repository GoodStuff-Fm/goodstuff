##
Episode № 7
Adam Clark


April 3rd, 2013
&middot;
58
minutes


Adam Clark joins me for episode 7 to talk about his new podcast, The Gently Mad, and where it came from. We talk about conducting an interview and why it matters if you do a podcast.


**Guest Starring:**
