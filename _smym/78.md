##
Episode № 78
Raygan Kelly and Nate Heininger of The Short Game


March 24th, 2015
&middot;
70
minutes


Raygan and Nate, two of four hosts of The Short Game - a podcast that talks about video games that ideally take less than 10 hours.


We talk about video games and what lead to the creation of The Short Game, how to have a good discussion with four people over Skype and the tips Raygan has for editing in Logic Pro X - something I recently upgraded to. Finally.
